Lingua

A language designed with beginners in mind.

Easy to read, write, and learn.

First developed at HackSB 2018

Rationale

Lingua was built on three principles: that a language should be easy to read, easy to write, and easy to fix your mistakes in. Too many times, students get frustrated or give up because they do not understand the complex, often esoteric nature of many programming languages. After all, many modern languages were designed with experienced developers in mind, not beginners. Lingua attempts to fill this void by providing a language designed specifically with beginners in mind. With easy and clean syntax that makes sense, and compiler errors that give helpful tips on how to fix mistakes, Lingua is the best language for beginners to pick up and start learning right now.

Features

As of May 2019, Lingua is still very much a work-in-progress. While most of the syntax and semantics parsing has been completed, the compiler itself has not been written. Click here to visit the Git repo for Lingua.
